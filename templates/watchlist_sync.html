How it works
  • Every 5 minutes this app polls your Plex watchlist via Plex's cloud API.
  • New TV shows are looked up and added to Sonarr with all seasons monitored and an immediate episode search triggered.
  • New movies are looked up and added to Radarr with an immediate download search triggered.
  • Quality profile: prefers any profile with 1080p in the name, falls back to 720p, then the first available profile.
  • Root folder: the folder with the most free space is chosen automatically.
  • Each item is only processed once — once it's marked "added" or "already exists" it won't be retried on future syncs.
  • Items that fail will be retried on the next sync run (or you can retry them manually below).
